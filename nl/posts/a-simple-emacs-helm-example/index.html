A simple Emacs helm example

Daniel Nicolai

2020-09-01 17:05

Commentaar

Although I recommend Ivy over Helm for most people, because Ivy's use and documentation is much more approachable, there might be some time that you can not avoid using Helm. I find Helm's documentation quite cryptic, with very few examples. Therefore here follow two simple examples to help you out quickly. 

(defun helm-print ()
  (interactive)
    (helm :sources (helm-build-sync-source "Name"
		     :candidates '(a b c)
		     :action '(("print" . (lambda (x)
					     (print x)))))))
